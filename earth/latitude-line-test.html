Latitude Line Test

In this UI experiment the Earth is stationary, tilted and illuminated as if it was June 21. Your location is what changes when either arrow-keys or dragging is used.

Either drag with the mouse or use the arrows keys to change the location you are viewing the Earth from. Use the command or control keys along with the up or down arrows to move closer or further away from the Earth Use the command or control keys along with the left or right arrows to move change the direction you are facing.

Use shift and up or down arrow to adjust the latitude line.
